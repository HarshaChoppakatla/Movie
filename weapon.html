Weapon



Weapon is a 2024 Indian superhero film directed by Guhan Senniappan and produced by M. S. Manzoor under Million Studio. The film stars Sathyaraj and Vasanth Ravi, alongside Rajiv Menon, Tanya Hope, Syed Subahan And Rajeev Pillai.

Release date: 7 June 2024 (India)

Director: Guhan Senniappan

Budget: 90 crore INR

Languages: Tamil, Hindi

Music director: Ghibran

Producer: MS Manzoor
